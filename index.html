TEAM 6934

ABOUT US

Team 6934 is the robotics team of Adolfo Camarillo High School. Since 2018, we have been dedicated to teaching high schoolers skills in computer science, engineering, design, and business. We work with industry professionals and FRC alumni to foster a competitive FRC team for competition and beyond.

FIRST

FIRST—or For Inspiration in Science and Technology—is a non-profit organization for high schoolers around the world. More than just a robotics competition, FIRST aims to teach teens the merits of technology, gracious professionalism, and collaboration.
